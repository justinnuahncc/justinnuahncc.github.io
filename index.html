Workforce Data & HR Decision Support

What We Do

Charleswood Consulting provides workforce data and HR decision support to small and mid-sized organizations. We help leadership teams better organize, interpret, and use their existing HR data to make clearer decisions around staffing, cost, utilization, and workforce risk.

Our work is project-based and executive-focused—designed to add senior-level HR perspective without adding headcount or changing systems.

How We Help

Organizations often have the data they need, but not the time or perspective to turn it into decision-ready insight. Charleswood Consulting bridges that gap by providing structured analysis and practical guidance leaders can act on.

Typical focus areas include:

  • Workforce and headcount analysis
  • Labor cost and utilization trends
  • Turnover, retention, and risk indicators
  • Organizational structure and role clarity review
  • HR data cleanup and decision-ready reporting

Engagement Model

  • Project-based or limited ongoing support
  • Works alongside internal HR, Finance, or Operations
  • Complements (does not replace) HRIS or analytics vendors
  • No system implementations or HR operations ownership

Who It’s For

  • CEOs, CFOs, COOs, and senior leaders
  • Organizations without in-house workforce analytics
  • Teams seeking clarity before growth, restructuring, or cost decisions
  • Leaders who value experienced HR judgment grounded in data
